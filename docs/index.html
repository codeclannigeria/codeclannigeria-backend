The official website for code clan nigeria community We are a community comprised of future innovators. Front-end developers, DevOps engineers, full stack developers, technical writers, software engineers. This community was created out of a desire to provide ongoing support to you wherever stage you are in your coding career.

This is codeclannigeria.dev

As part of the measures towards achieving out goal stated above, we propose to bring together the amazing talents of our community members to built a website and a mobile application that represents all that we stand for.

How You Come In

Feel free to put your skills to the test. Here is a suggested workflow for you to follow: Note: This is applicable to codeclannigeria community members only.

  1. Fork this repository into your local computer
  2. Check the issues pane for an issue that needs to be solved
  3. Find an issue that matches your current skill level and solve it
  4. Make your pull request to the master branch
  5. Wait for some feedback.
  6. Repeat the process

Want more support? Join our slack community and ask questions.

Thanks for your support
